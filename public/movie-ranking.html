My top 3 series of all time

Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is an American fantasy drama television series
created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss for HBO.
This is the best storytelling series of all time
which i love and also the cast displays.

Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is an American neo-Western
crime drama television series created and
produced by Vince Gilligan for AMC.
When we talk about crime and law,
this is definitely top tier stuff.

BMF(Black Mafia Family)

BMF (or Black Mafia Family) is an American crime
drama television series created by Randy Huggins,
which follows the Black Mafia Family,
a drug trafficking and money laundering organization
When i got the recommendation, i had no damn idea
of how good this series is.
